## Rules

- First chapter: omit Previous. Last: omit Next or link to `../sources.md`.  
- Relative paths only.  
- Nav at top and bottom.  
- Prefer **reused lesson maps** for geography; story art for narrative moments.  
- No `❓` guiding-question blockquotes (lesson pack style).  
- End non-final chapters with a forward hook in the last paragraph (not a labeled “Next:” pedagogy line unless it reads as natural prose).
